Who we are & What we do?

STANFACTORY IS AN EMERGING E-COMMERCE PLATFORM PROVIDING THE BEST QUALITY FASHION PRODUCTS AT AN AFFORDABLE PRICE FOR BOTH MEN AND WOMEN.

STANFACTORY has come a long way from its beginnings. Started in 2016 as a small fun BUSINESS in the name ROZALIO, which later in 2018 became a clothing brand named ROZALIO FASHION and that fun business changed into a serious and an emerging E-COMMERCE Store named "STANFACTORY". When founder first started out, with zero sales for few weeks the success in the passion for GETTING THE BEST PRODUCTS AT THE BEST PRICE drove them forward to have a customer family including more than 2000 people.
